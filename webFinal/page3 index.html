Destiny is easily one of my favorite games I have ever played. You may have read about this game in the about me section. It was so fun to play through the game and earn great loot. the best feeling was getting cool armor or weapons and take to fight other players online

Black Ops 2

Black ops 2 is my favorite game of the Black Ops franchise. Sure some of the other games that were made could be good, but Black Ops 2 had the best gameplay for zombies and multiplayer. It was just so much fun to get on and play this game with your friends
