Homelessness & Starvation

What is it?

Homelessness is pretty self explanitory. It is when someone doesn't have a home to live in and they're living under bad circumstances. Starvation is when someone doesn't have enough food available to survive.

How does this affect people?

Homelessness affects on average 554,000 poeple annually just in america. There are even more world wide. This means that these people don't have a stable home or home at all to live in. This is able to affect their health. Prblems such as heart diseases and skin disorders can begin to appear in the person. These are caused by the unsanitary environment that they're forced to live in. There is also prejudice against the homeless. This affects the ability for them to recieve help.

How does Starvation affect people?

It has been estimated that around 25,000 people die from starvation.(Was reported by The United Nations in 2012) Starvation leads to many health issues.This can include failure of organs or deteriation of one's body systems. If the person that is starving is able to begin eating again, they eouldn't be able to eat much and would have a restricted diet.This is due to the fact that the body isn't used to consuming food. If a person is unable to eat after a certain amount a time, they will no longer be able to survive.

What is being done and how can you help?

There are many non-profit organizations that are helping homeless and starving people. They have homeless centers where they provide a place to live and food to the homeless. There are other organizations that go around the world bringing food to places that don't have access to food. You can also help people that are in need. 1 way to help is by volunteering at an organization that helps the homeless and starving people. You help by assisting in giving necessities to the homeless. You can also give food to those who have nothing to eat.
